Curry

Description

Curry is a favorful saucy dish that is commonly associated with South Asian cuisine. With how simple it is to make and with numerous variations on the
ingredients to cook with, curry is a versatile dish that can easily be made to satisfy any particular taste. The following recipe is just one take on a curry recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 onions
  • 2 carrots
  • 3 Yukon gold potatoes
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1.5 lbs of chicken thighs
  • 1.5 tsp of oil
  • 4 cups of chicken stock/broth
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 package fo curry roux

Steps

  1. Peel and cut onions into wedges
  2. Peel and cut carrots
  3. Peel and cut potatoes
  4. Grate ginger
  5. Mince garlic
  6. Cut fat of chicken thighs and cut into bite-sized pieces. Optionally season with ground black pepper.
  7. Add oil to a pot on medium heat and add the onions
  8. Cook and saute the onions until they are tender and translucent
  9. Add minced garlic and grated ginger
  10. Add chicken and cook until meat is no longer pink. Turn heat down to prevent the onions from browning quickly.
  11. Add chicken stock to the pot
  12. Add soy sauce, carrots, and potatoes
  13. Cover the pot and simmer on medium-low heat for 15 minutes or until carrots and potatoes are cooked through
  14. Use a fine-mesh strainer to skim the fat off the surface
  15. Add in 1-2 cubes of curry roux at a time until curry reaches desirable thickness
  16. Stir the pot frequently and let the curry simmer uncovered for 5-10 minutes
  17. Serve with rice once finished
